How to Use

Step 1: Install the Extension

Go to the Chrome Web Store and search for "Quick Google Access". Click on "Add to Chrome" to install the extension.

Step 2: Activate the Extension

Once installed, click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar to activate it.

Step 3: Use the Shortcut

Now, whenever you want to open the first Google search result, simply press the shortcut keys: Alt+O (Windows) or Command+O (Mac).
